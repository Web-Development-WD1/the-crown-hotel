Conveniently Located Norwich Hotel

The Crown Hotel is a new independent hotel situated in the western edge of Norwich. Our hotel is conveniently located close to Norfolk and Norwich University Hospital. It is well-served by public transport links, and Norwich city centre is only a short bus ride away, making it the ideal base for exploring our fine city.

We offer clean, modern rooms that are perfect for business or leisure travel. Each room is equipped with comfortable beds, an en-suite bathroom, a television, a kettle and tea and coffee making facilities. We pride ourselves on our service and would be happy to assist you with anything you need during your stay.
