Our Facilities

The Gym

Our on-site gym offers a fully-equipped gym suite, showers, and a steam room. Guests can benefit from a variety of facilities such as cardio equipment, strength and resistance kits, as well as a range of fitness classes and personal trainers.

The Bar

The perfect place for a relaxing drink with family and friends. Our bar offers a range of local beers on tap, wines, spirits and hot drinks such as coffee, teas and hot chocolate. Drinks are served from 11am - 11pm daily.

The Restaurant

Enjoy exquisite cuisine and expertly paired wine at our restaurant. Savour a variety of cuisines including modern British, classic French as well as our award-winning vegetarian and vegan menus. Open for breakfast, brunch, lunch and dinner.

The Function Room

We have three function rooms available for private hire. Our function rooms are perfect for all occasions, from wedding celebrations to birthday parties as well as for formal meetings. The rooms can accommodate up to 60 people. Please enquire at reception if you would like to hire the function rooms.
