Frequently Asked Questions

Booking Process

How far in advance can I book?

You can make bookings up to 12 months in advance.

How do I book more than one room?

You can select the number of rooms using the drop-down menu next to each room on our booking page.

Is my booking guaranteed?

Your card details provided at the time of booking guarantees your booking. You can use your booking reference to view your booking once it has been made. To do this, click the Manage Booking link at the top of the website.

How do I know if my booking is confirmed?

You will receive a booking confirmation, including your booking reference. You can use your booking reference to view your booking details.

How do I change my booking?

You can change your booking by entering your booking reference on our manage booking page.

How do I cancel my booking?

You can cancel your booking by entering your booking reference on our manage booking page.

About your stay

What is the check-in and check-out times?

You can check in any time after 3 p.m., and check out any time before 12 p.m..

Can I extend my stay?

Please speak to reception if you would like to extend your stay. The receptionist may be able arrange this for you, but this is not guaranteed, especially during busy seasons.

Are there accessible rooms?

The ground floor rooms have wheelchair access. Please contact us if you would like any special accessibility arrangements during your stay.

Hotel information

Is there a restaurant at the hotel?

Yes - we have an award-winning restaurant serving breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Are pets allowed in the hotel?

Unfortunately we are not able to accommodate pets due to hygiene concerns.

Is there parking at the hotel?

Yes - you are welcome to use our customer car park for the duration of your stay

Can I arrange airport transfers with the hotel?

Please contact us if you would like us to book a taxi for you.
